17 To use this project, follow these steps:

19 1. Create your working environment.

23 ## Creating Your Project
24 
25 Using this template to create a new Django app is easy::
26 
27  $ django-admin.py startproject --template=https://github.com/heroku/heroku-django-template/archive/master.zip --name=Procfile helloworld
28 
29 (If this doesn't work on windows, replace `django-admin.py` with `django-admin`)
30 
31 You can replace ``helloworld`` with your desired project name.
